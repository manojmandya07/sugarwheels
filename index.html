Frequently asked questions

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Reach out to our customer support team.

How can I book a cycle?
To book a cycle, simply download our application and create an account. Then, navigate to the booking section and select the desired cycle and duration. Finally, proceed to make the payment.
What are the available rental durations?
We offer both hourly and daily rental options. You can choose the duration that suits your needs.
Can I cancel my booking?
Yes, you can cancel your booking. However, please note that cancellation fees may apply depending on the timing of the cancellation. Refer to our cancellation policy for more details.
Is there a deposit required?
Yes, we require a refundable deposit to ensure the safe return of the cycle. The deposit amount will be specified during the booking process.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards and digital wallets. You can choose the most convenient option for you.
